The Villa

Comfort, framed
by the hills.

Nestled amidst lush greenery and scenic mountain views, Harshavardhan Villa offers a tranquil, self-contained stay just twenty minutes from Imagica. Warm, modern interiors meet an easy outdoor rhythm — a large lounge, a working kitchen, and a spacious sit-out patio that spills into the garden.

A short walk from the revered Ballaleshwar Ashtavinayak temple, the villa suits both a quiet family weekend and a slower, reflective retreat — with a caretaker close by, but never intrusive.
